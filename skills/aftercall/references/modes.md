# Modes

## `work-call`

Best for:
- team syncs
- client calls
- planning sessions
- internal operations calls
- sales / discovery calls

Default output shape:
- what was discussed
- decisions
- owners
- next steps
- blockers / open questions

## `therapy-session`

Best for:
- therapy sessions
- coaching calls
- personal reflection conversations
- emotionally meaningful one-on-ones

Default output shape:
- key themes
- what especially surfaced
- patterns / tensions
- what to reflect on between sessions
- one practical next step

Do not diagnose. Keep language careful.

## `client-follow-up`

Best for:
- post-call email drafts
- short recap messages
- client next-step confirmations
- scope / expectation alignment after a meeting

Default output shape:
- 2-4 bullet recap
- next steps
- ready-to-send message

## `study-call`

Best for:
- tutoring calls
- study groups
- mentoring sessions
- lecture debriefs
- educational coaching

Default output shape:
- what was covered
- key concepts
- what needs review
- open questions
- next study steps
